Meet Dr. Anushree Baishya

M.B.B.S, M.D Dermatology | Reg no. 16819 (AMC)

Dr. Anushree Baishya is a highly acclaimed dermatologist based in Guwahati, Assam, with more than two decades of experience in clinical and aesthetic skin care.

Her global expertise includes specialized training in Aesthetic Dermatology from the National Skin Centre, Singapore. She is an active voice in the medical community, regularly sharing her insights through presentations at national and international platforms like state conferences, national conferences, and prestigious international meets such as Dubai Derma.
