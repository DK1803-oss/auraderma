AURRADERMA SKIN CLINIC
led by Dr Anushree Baishya, M.B.B.S | MD (Dermatology)

With over 21 years of dedicated experience in dermatology and dermatosurgery, Dr. Anushree Baishya is a leading skin specialist based in Guwahati. A graduate of Gauhati Medical College in dermatology from post graduate Assam Medical College, she further honed her expertise with advanced training at the National Skin Centre in Singapore.

Dr. Baishya specializes in treating complex clinical skin conditions, vitiligo surgery, and advanced cosmetic procedures. Her practice is built on a foundation of clinical excellence and a commitment to utilizing the latest global dermatological advancements to provide her patients with safe, effective, and transformative results.

Personalized, Evidence-Based Care

No two skins are the same. Dr. Baishya is known for her meticulous, patient-centric approach, creating customized treatment plans that prioritize long-term skin health over quick fixes.
